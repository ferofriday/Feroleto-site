About

I'm an organic grower based in Fairfield County, Connecticut. I run Feroleto Garden Co., where I take care of gardens for a small number of residential clients — raised beds, seasonal planting, hands-on maintenance. I also co-manage the market garden at Wakeman Town Farm, an educational homestead in Westport where we grow vegetables, cut flowers, and run a farmstand.

Feroleto Garden Co.

Organic garden maintenance for residential clients across Fairfield County. I work with a small number of properties — visiting regularly, building and maintaining raised beds, planning seasonal rotations, and keeping gardens productive and beautiful year-round. Everything I use is organic and OMRI-listed.

Serving Fairfield County, CT

Wakeman Town Farm

I co-manage the market garden at Wakeman Town Farm, an educational nonprofit homestead in Westport. We grow mixed vegetables and cut flowers, run a farmstand, and work with volunteers and interns to teach sustainable growing practices.
